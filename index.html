WEB DEVELOPMENT

Web development encompasses the process of creating websites or web applications that are accessible via the internet. It involves a combination of various technologies and skills. At its core, web development typically involves three main components: HTML (HyperText Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and JavaScript..

HTML provides the structure of a web page, defining its elements such as headings, paragraphs, images, and links. CSS is used to style these elements, controlling aspects like layout, colors, fonts, and spacing to enhance the visual presentation of the page. JavaScript adds interactivity to web pages, enabling features such as form validation, animations, and dynamic content updates.

LASER CUTTINIG

Laser cutting is a highly precise manufacturing process that employs a concentrated laser beam to cut through various materials with accuracy and efficiency. It operates by directing the laser beam onto the material's surface, causing it to melt, burn, or vaporize, resulting in clean and precise cuts. This technology finds extensive use across industries for cutting metals, plastics, wood, and more, catering to diverse applications like signage, automotive parts, and intricate designs. Laser cutting offers advantages such as minimal material wastage, high speed, and the ability to handle complex designs. Safety measures, including protective eyewear and ventilation, are essential due to the high-intensity laser beams involved.
